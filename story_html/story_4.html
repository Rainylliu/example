Water-damaged computer

I dreamed that a glass of water fell down, and the water splashed on my computer, and the computer screen went straight black. I immediately realized that my computer might have been compromised, and that my recent homework was only half done and had not been saved. I felt a strong sense of tension and anxiety and was in a very anxious mood. I quickly wiped off the water and tried to save it. However, despite all my efforts, it still wouldn't start. I hurried out of the house and went to the nearest computer repair shop.

After the staff at the repair shop checked my computer, they told me it was in bad shape and it could take weeks to fix. They advised me to consider buying a new computer, but unfortunately they didn't have the model I needed and I would have to wait at least two weeks if I wanted to buy one. I was in a very anxious mood because my homework was due soon.
